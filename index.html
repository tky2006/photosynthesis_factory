📖 How to Play

🎯 Step-by-Step Guide

  1. 1Look at the Target Glucose amount at the top-right of the factory.
  2. 2Drag SUNLIGHT ☀️, WATER 💧, and CO₂ 🧪 icons into the glowing chloroplast.
  3. 3The factory needs 6 CO₂ + 6 H₂O + 1 Sunlight to produce 1 Glucose + 6 O₂.
  4. 4Watch the Production Bar – it fills as you add the correct materials!
  5. 5When the bar reaches full, the factory automatically produces Glucose 🍬.
  6. 6Keep producing until you hit the target before the ⏱ 20-second timer runs out!

💡 Game Tips

  • Remember the formula: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂
  • Don't overload the factory – it can only hold so many materials!
  • Sunlight is the energy source – you always need it to start production.
  • Watch the resource counters – you have limited materials per round!
  • In later rounds, avoid DECOY materials like Nitrogen – they waste capacity!
